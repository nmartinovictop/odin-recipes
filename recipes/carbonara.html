Carbonara

Description

Colloquially known as "Coal Miners" pasta, this basic dish is delicious and easy

Ingredients

  • 100g pasta (any similar to spaghetti will work)
  • 100g Guanciale (Bacon or Pancetta wil also work well)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. Start by cooking the pasta
  2. While the pasta is cooking, cook the guanciale in a pan. Once crispy, turn pan off to let cool.
  3. Add parmesean, salt & pepper, 1 whole egg, and 1 egg yolk to a mixing bowl. Mix well until the mixture has a creamy consistency
  4. Once the pasta is done, add it to the guanciale. Mix well.
  5. Add the egg/paremsean mixture. Add slowly while mixing. We do not want the heat of the pasta or pan to scramble the egg but instead turn it into a custardy sauce
  6. Serve in a bowl. Free to top with more cheese.
