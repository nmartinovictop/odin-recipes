Garlic Noodles

Description

These garlic noodles are delicous - savory and salty and garlicky and incredible.

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles
  • 3 tbs parmesean cheese, grated
  • 7 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sause
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce

Steps

  1. Begin by cooking your noodles in a shallow saucepan
  2. Add butter and minced garlic to a saucepan. Cook until butter is melted and garlic is fragrant, but not brown
  3. Once pasta is ready, add to butter/garlic mixture. No need to strain the pasta. Any water will help emulsify the liquid. Mix well.
  4. Add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and paremsean chease. Mix well.
  5. Serve pasta!
