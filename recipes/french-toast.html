French Toast

Description

French toast is a great way to take advantage of old bread

Ingredients

  • 2 slices breadbread
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Optional: cinnamon, allspice, vanilla

Steps

  1. Combine and mix milk, egg, sugar, and optional spices in a mixing bowl until it has a custardy like texture
  2. Soak bread in egg mixture for 15 minutes, alternating sides
  3. In a frying pan, melt your butter
  4. Place bread in frying pan until cooked, approximately 4 minutes each side
